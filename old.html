Welcome to the Norfolk Gazelles

We are a friendly running club based in Norwich, Norfolk. We welcome all standards, from first-time runners to serious high-level athletes.

Our aim is to help you be the best runner you can be, through coaching encouragement and support.

If you decide to join us, you'll find the details on our membership page.

We offer potential new members up to a 1 month free trial to see if its for you.  

If you wish to book on a training session please email training@norfolkgazelles.co.uk including which session you would like to book on, current park run or 5k time and emergency contact details.
